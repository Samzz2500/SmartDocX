Common Questions

What happens when tokens run out?▼

You won't be able to process new documents until you upgrade. Your existing documents and history remain accessible.

Do tokens roll over?▼

No, tokens expire with the plan validity period. Unused tokens are forfeited at expiry.

Can I upgrade mid-plan?▼

Yes. Upgrading adds the new plan's tokens to your account and extends validity.

💡 Tokens are credited instantly after payment confirmation.
